Complaints and Violations in Emerald Equity housing - East Harlem

    We are looking at the number and types of complaints and violations in housing stock owned by Emerald Equity in East Harlem.

    By Deeksha Kalra, Georgia Kromrei, Jack Bai, Yu-Hsuan (Jessy)


    Some questions we are looking to answer after observing the data are:
    1. No. of complaints of the same buildings in East Harlem before and after Emerald Equity owned them

    2. No. of complaints for violations in Emerald Equity - East Harlem buildings compared to the rest of the city/ Manhattan?

    3. Highest number and types of violations in Emerald equity -East Harlem buildings compared to the rest of Manhattan? (gas, lead etc)

    4. Percentage of B class ( based on priority of attention needed) violations in Emerald equity-East Harlem buildings compared to the rest of Manhattan? (B class has 5529 violations out of 9391)
